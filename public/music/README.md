# Background Music

Place your music files here to add atmospheric background music to the game.

## File Names

The audio service will look for these files:
- `solar.mp3` or `solar.ogg` - Main exploration mode (ambient/atmospheric)
- `arcade.mp3` or `arcade.ogg` - Arcade asteroid shooter (energetic)
- `orbit.mp3` or `orbit.ogg` - Orbit simulator (calm/focused)
- `raiden.mp3` or `raiden.ogg` - Mars defense mode (intense)

## Supported Formats
- MP3 (best browser support)
- OGG (good quality, open format)
- WAV (larger files, best quality)

## Recommended Music Style
- **Solar Mode**: Ambient, spacey, atmospheric (think Stellaris, Mass Effect exploration)
- **Arcade Mode**: Upbeat, electronic, retro (think classic arcade games)
- **Orbit Mode**: Calm, focused, minimal (good for concentration)
- **Raiden Mode**: Intense, fast-paced (think bullet hell shooters)

## Where to Find Free Music
- [Incompetech (Kevin MacLeod)](https://incompetech.com/music/royalty-free/) - CC BY
- [FreePD](https://freepd.com/) - Public domain
- [Purple Planet](https://www.purple-planet.com/) - Free for non-commercial
- [OpenGameArt](https://opengameart.org/) - Various licenses

## Tips
- Keep file sizes reasonable (2-5 MB per track)
- Loop-friendly tracks work best
- Volume will be automatically controlled by the game's music volume setting
